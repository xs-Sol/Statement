xs-Sol Statement


Dear members and customers,


xs-Sol has been online since 2013. It has not only grown into a website providing tools and business solutions but also to a real community with over 10.000 customers and users combined. Some of the clients were high profile Instagram influencers with over 4 million followers as also corporations.

However, it developed to a scale which is not manageable with given manpower. The market with the latest technologies has increased massively and it has become vastly time-consuming operating xs-Sol.

Reluctantly, I've come to the decision to discontinue and shut down this venture.
Therefore, a big thank you to all the members and customers who trusted but also supported the idea during the last years!


Best regards,
Lars - Chief Executive Officer
